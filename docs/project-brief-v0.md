# gameAIgents — Project Brief v0

## Vision
Enable small teams to ship playable games faster using AI-native workflows without sacrificing creative control.

## Problem
Game development is slow and tool-fragmented:
- ideation, narrative, art, and prototyping happen in disconnected systems
- iteration cycles are expensive
- solo founders cannot cover all disciplines at production quality

## Target Users
- indie founders
- small distributed studios
- technically curious creators who can ship but need leverage

## Proposed Solution
gameAIgents provides a collaborative control center where specialized AI agents support:
- concept and genre exploration
- gameplay loop prototyping
- narrative/world consistency
- playtest analysis and balancing suggestions

## Success Metrics (MVP)
- time-to-first-playable < 48 hours
- 3+ iteration loops in week one per active team
- 30% reduction in discarded prototype effort

## Constraints
- creator IP ownership clarity
- safe/transparent use of AI-generated assets
- platform compliance (Steam disclosure, policy alignment)

## Open Questions
- first engine target for export
- pricing (credit vs seat)
- scope of multiplayer support in MVP
